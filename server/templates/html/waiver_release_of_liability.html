1. ACKNOWLEDGMENT OF RISK
I acknowledge that participating in activities offered by {{BusinessName}}, including but not limited to yoga, pilates, fitness classes, personal training, wellness coaching, workshops, retreats, online programs, and related physical activities (collectively, the "Activities"), involves inherent risks.
I understand that these risks include, but are not limited to:
  • Physical injury, including strains, sprains, fractures, or other musculoskeletal injuries;
  • Cardiovascular complications, including heart attack or stroke;
  • Exacerbation of pre-existing medical conditions;
  • Injuries from equipment, props, or facility conditions;
  • Injuries from interactions with other participants; and
  • In rare cases, permanent disability or death.
I acknowledge that these risks exist regardless of the care taken by {{BusinessName}}, its owners, instructors, employees, contractors, or agents.
2. ASSUMPTION OF RISK
I voluntarily choose to participate in the Activities with full knowledge of the risks involved. I expressly assume all risks associated with my participation, whether known or unknown, foreseen or unforeseen.
I understand that it is my responsibility to:
  • Consult with a physician before beginning any fitness or wellness program;
  • Disclose any medical conditions, injuries, or physical limitations that may affect my ability to safely participate;
  • Listen to my body and stop any activity that causes pain or discomfort;
  • Follow all instructions provided by instructors and staff; and
  • Use equipment and facilities safely and as intended.
3. RELEASE AND WAIVER OF LIABILITY
In consideration for being permitted to participate in the Activities, I hereby release, waive, discharge, and covenant not to sue {{BusinessName}}, {{LegalEntityName}}, its owners, officers, directors, employees, instructors, contractors, agents, representatives, successors, and assigns (collectively, the "Released Parties") from any and all liability, claims, demands, actions, or causes of action arising out of or related to any loss, damage, injury, or death that may be sustained by me while participating in the Activities, whether caused by the negligence of the Released Parties or otherwise.
This release applies to all claims, whether based on negligence, breach of contract, premises liability, or any other legal theory, and includes claims for personal injury, property damage, wrongful death, loss of consortium, and any other losses.
4. INDEMNIFICATION
I agree to indemnify, defend, and hold harmless the Released Parties from any and all claims, liabilities, damages, costs, and expenses (including attorney's fees) arising from my participation in the Activities, including but not limited to claims brought by third parties for injuries or damages I may cause.
5. MEDICAL TREATMENT AUTHORIZATION
In the event of a medical emergency, I authorize {{BusinessName}} and its staff to obtain or provide emergency medical treatment for me. I understand that I am solely responsible for all costs associated with such medical treatment.
I acknowledge that {{BusinessName}} does not provide medical or health care services and that all Activities are for general wellness and fitness purposes only.
6. PHOTO AND VIDEO CONSENT
I understand that {{BusinessName}} may take photographs or videos during classes, events, or Activities. I consent to the use of my image and likeness in promotional materials, social media, and marketing, unless I notify {{BusinessName}} in writing of my objection.
7. PERSONAL PROPERTY
I understand that {{BusinessName}} is not responsible for lost, stolen, or damaged personal property. I agree to keep my belongings secure and assume all risk of loss or damage to my personal items.
8. SEVERABILITY
If any provision of this Waiver is found to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.

Any disputes arising from this Waiver or my participation in the Activities shall be resolved exclusively in the courts located in {{Jurisdiction}}, and I consent to the personal jurisdiction of such courts.
